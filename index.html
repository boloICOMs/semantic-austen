The Project

Semantic Austen is a pioneering digital library project that seeks to bridge the gap between classic literature and modern computational linguistics. By applying granular semantic tagging to the complete works of Jane Austen, we aim to uncover the intricate social networks, subtle shifts in narrative perspective, and characteristic linguistic markers that define Austen's unique literary voice.

Our methodology combines rigorous scholar-led annotation with advanced natural language processing techniques, providing researchers and enthusiasts alike with new ways to navigate and understand some of the most beloved texts in the English language.
